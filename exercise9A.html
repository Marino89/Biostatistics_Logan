Fit the additive muliple linear model

loyn.lm<-lm(ABUND~log10(AREA)+YR.ISOL+log10(DIST)+log10(LDIST)+GRAZE+ALT,
            data=loyn)

Plotting

plot(loyn.lm)

  • Conclusions - There is no obvious Wedge pattern evident in the residual plot. none of the point approach the high Cooks D contours suggesting theat none of the observations are overly influential on the final fitted model.
